JSConf 2013

If you haven't been to a JSConf yet, I strongly encourage you to do so. From the organization of the event to the quality of the material to the great location selected, the conference has far exceeded what were already high expectations.

There were 3 concurrent tracks at the conference, so only those I attended myself are summarized here. Other attendees have summarized some of the talks I didn't get to due to scheduling conflicts - you can find those at jlongster.com (James Long) and Toby Ho's github repo.

If you love writing javascript, enjoy the thought of building client-side JS apps, working with a lot of node.js and doing awesome things for lots of customers on mobile devices, come work with me at Zappos! (And if you don't like the Mobile part of that, there's options for you too - in two locations!)
